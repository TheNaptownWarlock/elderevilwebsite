The Art of Coffee Roasting

May 15, 2024

Roasting coffee is both a science and an art. It's where the magic happens, transforming green coffee beans into the aromatic, flavorful beans we all know and love.

The Roasting Process

When I roast coffee, I focus on three main stages:

  • Drying Phase: The beans lose moisture and turn from green to yellow
  • First Crack: The beans expand and make a cracking sound, similar to popcorn
  • Development Phase: Where the real flavor development happens

My Approach

I prefer a medium-dark roast that brings out the natural sweetness while maintaining the bean's unique characteristics. For my recent Sidama roast, I focused on:

  • Careful temperature control
  • Monitoring the bean color
  • Listening for the perfect crack
  • Ensuring even development

The result? A rich, complex cup with notes of turbinado sugar and a smooth finish that lingers on the palate.
