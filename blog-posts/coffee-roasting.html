The Art of Coffee Roasting

May 15, 2024

My journey into coffee roasting began as a quest to understand the alchemy behind the perfect cup. It's not just about heating beans - it's about unlocking their hidden potential through careful temperature control and timing.

Key elements of a successful roast:

  • Bean selection: Choosing the right origin and quality
  • Temperature control: Managing the roast curve
  • Timing: Knowing when to stop the roast
  • Cooling: Properly cooling the beans to lock in flavors

Each roast is a unique experiment, and I'm constantly learning new techniques to bring out the best in every bean.
