Clay Sculpting Techniques

May 1, 2024

Creating my Lil Guys has been a journey of discovery and experimentation. Each character starts as a simple lump of clay and transforms into a unique personality through careful sculpting and attention to detail.

My Process

When creating a new character, I follow these steps:

  • Concept Development: Sketching and planning the character's design
  • Armature Building: Creating a wire frame for support
  • Basic Sculpting: Building up the main forms
  • Detail Work: Adding facial features and textures
  • Finishing: Smoothing and refining the surface

Character Creation

Each of my Lil Guys has their own story and personality:

  • The Jester: A playful character with exaggerated features and a mischievous smile
  • The Wizard: An elder figure with intricate details in his robes and staff

The key to bringing these characters to life is in the details - the way their expressions capture their personalities, and how their poses tell their stories.
