Clay Sculpting Techniques

May 1, 2024

Clay sculpting is a magical process that allows me to bring my imagination to life. Each piece starts as a simple lump of clay and transforms into something unique through careful shaping and detailing.

My process typically involves:

  • Sketching the initial concept
  • Building the basic form with clay
  • Adding details and textures
  • Firing and finishing the piece

Whether it's creating whimsical characters or more serious pieces, each sculpture tells its own story.
